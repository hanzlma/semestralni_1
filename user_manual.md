# Prsi Card Game - User manual

## How to play
1. Open a terminal in this directory.
2. Enter following command in the terminal: "python -m main"

## Selecting a difficulty
After turning the game on, you will be asked with choosing the difficulty of the game. There are two options:
1. Easy - the computer does not know the cards you have in your hand.
2. Hard - the computer know the cards you have in your hand.

## Available commands
1. help - lists all commands and explains their purpose.
2. list_cards (lc in abbr.) - lists all cards the player has in his hand
3. play_card (pc in abbr.) <\card> - play the card if can be played, says the card is unplayable otherwise
4. take_card (tc in abbr.) - takes a card
5. card_info (ci in abbr.) - describes all card colours and types
6. stand_round (sr in abbr.) - stands a round (can be only played if you are playing into an active ace card)
7. exit - exits the program
